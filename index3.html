We offer a wide variety including Pea, Radish, Sunflower, Wheatgrass, Broccoli, and Mustard microgreens. Each variety is grown fresh, chemical-free, and harvested at peak nutrition.

We offer daily fresh harvests and deliver every week within Kolkata. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before our daily cutoff time.
